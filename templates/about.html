About Us

The Smart Complaint Tracking System is a web-based platform designed to help users easily register and track complaints online. The system allows users to submit their issues and enables administrators to manage, monitor, and resolve complaints efficiently.

Our goal is to create a transparent and organized complaint management system that improves communication between users and administrators. The platform ensures that every complaint is recorded, tracked, and resolved in a timely manner.
